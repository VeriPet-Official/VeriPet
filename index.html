How VeriPet Works

From a single report to national enforcement — VeriPet connects every step with data, documentation, and legal power.

01

Report & Document

Public submits photos, videos, and reports. Every file is auto-timestamped with full chain-of-custody documentation — ready for legal action.

02

Verify & Investigate

Cases are prepared and made available to verified investigators and legal partners.

03

Enforce & Escalate

VeriPet supplies evidence and expert testimony — from education programs to felony charges and permanent bans.
