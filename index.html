Enforcement Gaps

Closing Critical Failures

Current animal welfare systems are reactive. We address the three critical failures in community pet management:

Accountability Gap

Lack of property-level accountability for pet ownership and resident responsibility.

Visibility Gap

No early warning system for risk patterns like overbreeding or neglect.

Data Gap

Fragmented documentation across shelters, complaints, and veterinary records.
