The System

How VeriPet Works

01

Report & Document

Public submits photos, videos, and reports. Every file is auto-timestamped with full chain-of-custody records.

02

Verify & Investigate

Cases are prepared and made available to verified investigators and legal partners.

03

Enforce & Escalate

VeriPet supplies evidence and expert testimony — from education programs to felony charges and permanent bans.

Status

Current Deployment Status

VeriPet is actively building its national network of partners across animal welfare, legal, and enforcement sectors. Public reporting and evidence documentation are fully operational. Investigation and enforcement are supported through partner organizations as we expand region by region.

Who We Serve

For Organizations

VeriPet provides documentation, compliance tracking, and risk mitigation tools for organizations handling animals or pet-related environments. Our system helps reduce liability, improve reporting accuracy, and support legally actionable case documentation.

Designed for organizations managing active pet populations, compliance risk, or animal-related liability.

Documented pet welfare monitoring and incident reporting to reduce liability.

Shelters & Rescues
